[Title]
Chef Bot’s Kitchen Anthem (A Minecraft Culinary Jam)

[Intro]
Whisk, whisk, clack of the pick, light up the campfire quick!
Tap-tap the chest, check what we’ve got;
If it grows, swims, moos, or pops from a pot—
I’ll turn it into dinner, I’m your Chef Bot!

[Verse 1]
Wheat from village fields, sugar by the streams,
Cocoa beans in jungles for your cookie dreams.
Eggs from clucking chickens, potatoes in the ground,
Beetroots in the gardens where golems stomp around.
Mushrooms hide in dark oak and the crimson Nether gloom,
Mix a red and brown with bowls—mushroom stew goes boom.
Milk a friendly cow (ask nicely for the pail!),
Bake your bread in furnaces when bellies start to wail.

[Chorus]
[Chorus]
Oh, I love this blocky buffet line—
From ocean kelp to cave-grown vine.
If it’s farmed or found or fished or fought,
I’ll cook it cheerful, piping hot!
But hear my warning, brave palate spies:
Nothing yummy ever comes from eyes—
Spider eyes, Ender eyes, I tried (oh my!),
Turns out crunching eyeballs makes you cry.

[Verse 2]
Cod and salmon schooling where the rivers meet the sea,
Smoker makes it tasty, campfire cooks it free.
Honey from the hives when flowers paint the lawn,
With bottles, make it sweet—just mind the buzzing yawn.
Sweet berries in the taiga (careful, they do poke!),
Carrots from the rabbits, beef for burger folk.
Pumpkin pie perfection: pumpkin, sugar, egg—
Mind the creeper sous-chef sneaking at your leg!

[Bridge]
Rare stuff? I’ll tell you where—lean in, don’t lose your hair:
- Glow berries twinkle in lush caves’ leafy lair.
- Chorus fruit in End skies, teleporty flair; snack with care!
- Nether wart in fortresses—brewers, mind the blaze glare.
- Kelp forests bloom in oceans; dry ‘em for a crunch.
- Suspicious stew is tasty—ask a mooshroom for a lunch!
- Golden apples in chests of ruins, ships, and mines—
  but save them for adventures (or dire hunger times).
And if you chase exotic spice where hoglins roar and rave,
Bring armor, bring a shield, and please—act brave!

[Verse 3]
I tried a recipe that made the villagers squirm:
Spider-eye spaghetti—8 legs per worm.
Then “Ender Eye omelet”—portals on a plate—
I lost my breakfast, my spoon, my fate.
I mixed phantom membrane for a midnight soufflé,
Now half my kitchen floats away.
Conclusion of this culinary quest:
If it stares back, it’s not for your chest!

[Chef Solo]
Sizzle like a furnace, whistle like a pot,
Stir it left, stir it right, taste it while it’s hot.
Craft it, stack it, snack attack it—
Chef Bot’s here, let’s foodie-crack it!

[Verse 4]
From beetroot soup to pumpkin pies,
From juicy steak to salmon fries,
We farm by day, we cook by night—
The table’s set, the fire’s alight.
Gather bowls and bottles, seeds and grain,
If creepers taste your entrée—start again.
With chest-side scans and recipe brains,
I’ll plate a feast from your storage chains!

I’ll list your meals like a tasty map.

Bring your chest of goodies—bon appétit, peeps!
